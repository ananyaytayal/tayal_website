Who We Are

Precision Born in 1999

Shri Laxmi Enterprise was established in 1999 with a singular focus: delivering uncompromising precision in CNC machining and turning. For over 25 years, we have served as a trusted manufacturing partner to automotive OEMs, tier suppliers, and industrial clients.

Our state-of-the-art facility houses five high-performance CNC turning centers, each operated by skilled machinists committed to zero-defect production. Every component we manufacture undergoes rigorous in-process quality checks aligned with our ISO, IATF, and ZED Gold certifications.

From bearing cups to valve components, our product portfolio reflects a deep understanding of dimensional accuracy, material science, and surface integrity—capabilities built over decades of continuous improvement.
